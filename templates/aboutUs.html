Chef Profile - Samuel Dhanasingh (Sam)

Sam grew up in busy and beautiful city madurai, India. Madurai is a city where you can get fresh and tasty food 24*7. This city never sleeps and has an unique traditional indian food. He grew up cooking in his family kitchen, watching his grandmother, mother and relatives cook, he carried this passion for fresh authentic cooking from his city and home kitchen, through a four-year engineering degree, then a two year masters degree at Melbourne University. He began working in various restaurants while in college in Melbourne. Where he discovered his passion to be a professional Chef and here i am. Thanks for visiting us.!!! sam@samscurry.com
